# About

I am a hands-on developer with a bias toward clarity, product thinking, and maintainable systems.

My best work usually sits at the intersection of frontend architecture, editorial UX, and technical storytelling. I care about how a site feels to scan, how quickly it loads, and how easy it is to evolve later.

## How I work

- I start from content structure, not visual decoration.
- I prefer systems that make future updates cheaper.
- I value explicit contracts between content, UI, and data.
- I avoid adding backend complexity unless it solves a real need.

## Strengths

- Frontend architecture and component systems
- Content modeling and MDX workflows
- Performance and SEO hygiene
- Technical writing and case study structure

## Stack I am comfortable with

Next.js, TypeScript, React, MDX, Tailwind CSS, accessibility-first UI patterns, content validation, and production deployment workflows.
