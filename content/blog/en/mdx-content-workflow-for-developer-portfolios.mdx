# MDX Content Workflow for Developer Portfolios

MDX is a good fit for portfolios because it gives you two things at once: structured content and enough flexibility to write like a human.

## The content model

I prefer to split content into clear buckets:

- Static pages
- Projects
- Blog posts
- Site settings

Each bucket gets a schema. Each schema gets explicit fields. That removes guesswork and keeps the site easier to evolve.

## Why this is better than ad hoc pages

When content lives inside page components, every new page becomes a small rewrite problem.

When content lives in MDX with validation, the UI can stay focused on layout while the content stays reusable.

## What a strong workflow gives you

- Faster publishing
- Cleaner bilingual support
- Better SEO metadata control
- Easier review and maintenance
