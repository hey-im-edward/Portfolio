# Project overview

This is the design layer behind a content-heavy portfolio: typography, rhythm, spacing, and reusable section logic.

## Problem

Content-heavy pages often become visually noisy when every block is treated like a card. The result is harder to scan and harder to maintain.

## Approach

I designed around editorial hierarchy first, then filled in reusable components only where they improved the flow.

## Key decisions

- Use typography and spacing as the primary layout tools.
- Keep motion subtle and purposeful.
- Optimize for long-form reading and quick scanning.

## Results

- More consistent pages
- Less UI clutter
- Easier long-form reading
- Better scalability for future content types
